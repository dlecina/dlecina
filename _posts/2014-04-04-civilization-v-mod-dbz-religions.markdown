This mod adds religions based on Dragon Ball Z, including: 

* Beerus Belief 
* Buudhism 
* Dao of Roshi 
* Eternal Dragon Enlightenment 
* Ginyu Dance Ritual 
* Kaio-ken 
* Mr. Satanism 
* Pecking Order 
* Power Level Philosophy 
* Saiyan Faith 
* Tiengriism 

This mod requires the Gods & Kings and/or Brave New World expansions.
